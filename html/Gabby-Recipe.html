Fettuccine Alfredo

A Recipe by The Suburban soapbox's chief Kellie: https://thesuburbansoapbox.com/the-very-best-fettuccine-alfredo-recipe/

Ingredients:

  • 8oz Fettuccine egg pasta
  • 4 cups of water
  • 8 tbps Salted butter
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1 cup half and half
  • juice and Zest of one lemon
  • 2 cups Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/8 Nutmeg
  • Salt and Ground pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley to serve

Equipment:

  • Large pot
  • Large Saucepan
  • Wooden Spoon
  • timer

Directions:

  1. Grab the large pot and fill it up with salted water. then bring it to a medium boil.
  2. Pour the pasta into a large pot with boiling water and follow the designated instructions on the pasta box
  3. In a large saucepan melt butter over mediun heat.
  4. Pour and whisk heavy cream and half and half into the large pot with butter. let the sauce heat to a simmer. afterwards remove from heat and whisk in lemon juice and zest.
  5. Add 1 1/2 cups of cheese into the sauce, stir until smooth. then include nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste
  6. Add the cooked pasta into the sauce and pour in remainding cheese. toss to coat until the sauce thickens.
  7. Chop parsley and sprinkle on fettuccine, it's ready to serve.
