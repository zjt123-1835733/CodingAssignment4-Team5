Chicken Milanesas

Video o referencia: Ver contenido

Required ingredients

  • Chicken fillets: 4–6 boneless, skinless breasts (500–700 g).
  • Wheat flour: 2 tablespoons up to 100 g.
  • Eggs: 2–3, beaten (optional: 1 cup milk).
  • Breadcrumbs: 200–270 g (traditional or panko).
  • Salt and pepper: to taste.
  • Oil or butter: enough to fry until golden.

Necessary tools to make a chicken Milanese

  1. Non-stick pan: prevents sticking and eases cleaning.
  2. Kitchen thermometer: controls oil temperature.
  3. Kitchen tongs: to turn fillets safely.
  4. These tools help achieve a crispy and tender Milanese.
